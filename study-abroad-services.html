FAQ’S

Frequently Asked Quetions

  • Which countries can I study in?
    We guide students to top destinations like the UK, USA, Canada, Dubai, Europe, and New Zealand
  • Is IELTS mandatory for all countries?
    No. Some countries like Dubai and parts of Europe accept MOI (Medium of Instruction) instead of IELTS.
  • What is the minimum academic percentage needed?
    Usually 50–60%. Some UK universities accept 45%+.
  • Can I study with a gap in my education?
    Yes, with a valid reason like work experience or medical history.
  • Can I work while studying?
    Yes, most countries allow part-time work during studies.
